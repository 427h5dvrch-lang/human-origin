# HumanOrigin
## Prove you actually worked on your document.

HumanOrigin observes your activity while you work and cryptographically links it to your final document. You get a portable proof anyone can verify.

**[Download for macOS]** · [See an example proof]

### How it works

1. **Choose your document** — HumanOrigin starts tracking it.
2. **Work normally** — it measures your work process.
3. **Create your proof** — a signed kit, ready to send and verify.

### What HumanOrigin proves — and what it does not

✅ That a human work process was observed on this document.
✅ That the document was modified during that observation.

❌ HumanOrigin **does not detect AI**.
❌ It **does not certify originality or factual truth** of the content.
❌ It **does not prove that you are the sole author**.

### Privacy

HumanOrigin processes the document locally to compute fingerprints and change indicators. The document's content is not sent to HumanOrigin, and HumanOrigin does not judge what you wrote.

*HumanOrigin proves you worked on this document — not what you wrote.*
