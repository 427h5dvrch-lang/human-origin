# Invitation — HumanOrigin Beta Test (macOS)

Hi,

I'm reaching out because I'm working on a tool and would genuinely value your feedback.

**HumanOrigin** is a macOS app that quietly records your keyboard and mouse activity while you work, then produces a verifiable proof that the work was done by a human — tied to a specific document.

It is not an AI detector. The app has no way of knowing whether you used AI, and doesn't try to. What it attests is that a real human work process took place, over real time, on your machine.

---

**What I'm asking:**

Install the app, do a short work session — write a few paragraphs, annotate a document, draft something — then generate a HumanOrigin document from that session.

Estimated time: **15 to 20 minutes**, including installation.

---

**What I need from you:**

Not polite feedback — honest feedback. If something is confusing, unconvincing, or hard to use, that's exactly what I need to hear before a wider release.

I'm attaching an installation guide and a simple test scenario. There's a short questionnaire at the end.
